About

Hi, I am a first year PhD student in Econometrics and Statistics at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business, supervised by Bryon Aragam and Dacheng Xiu. Before joining Booth, I earned my MS in Statistics at UChicago, advised by Jingshu Wang. Prior to that, I obtained my BS in Statistics from Renmin University of China and was working with Xiaoling Lu and Danhui Yi. I grew up in Fuzhou, Fujian.
